Manivest Value Fund


Manivest is an AI-Powered Alternative Investment Fund investing in global equities based on a rigorous and rule-based investment strategy. Utilizing advanced Machine Learning, our investment software calculates in real-time the optimal time for buying or selling an asset. When an asset is selected for trading, the trading is to 90% autonomous, and the strategy applied is a Machine Learning based Swing-Trading.

I worked on the Manivest platform for a couple of years, during my time living in Texas and Antigua. This was my first experience working with a large project and this gave me a lot of overviewing capabilities to manage and create large scale applications. My responsibillies at Manivest included everything from Market screening as Junior Analyst, to coding and designing UI/UX as well as the backend.

Manivest is an AI-Powered Alternative Investment Fund investing in global equities based on a rigorous and rule-based investment strategy. Kolbytes helped develop and design UI/UX and the backend.

GlassOrTwo

Minimize Wine Waste
& Open The Bottles You Never Would


GlassOrTwo is a mobile application we are developing. The application will allow users to find wine deals from restaurants around their area. It will also create a possibility for restaurants to open and sell special and expensive bottles, per glass. The restaurants will be able to send a deal out and the price of the deal will follow the time left of the life span on the opened bottle. The application is made with Flutter and is still under construction.
The app showcased here is only a responsive visualization of the actual app.
